# Claim Coverage & Processing Rules

## Policy Validity
- Claim admissible only if the incident occurs within the policy validity dates.
- Claims filed after 30 days of incident require special approval.

## Claim Coverage
- Comprehensive policies cover third-party + own-damage.
- Third-party policies do NOT cover own-damage.

## Claim Approval Criteria
- Claimed amount ≤ Sum Insured.
- Valid documents: FIR, Repair Invoice, Photos.
- Vehicle must not be used for commercial purposes.

## Escalation Criteria
- High claim amount (> ₹25,000)
- Suspicious or missing documents
- Multiple claims within last 6 months
